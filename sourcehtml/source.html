DRIVING DYNAMICS

Rear-Wheel Steering: Enhances agility and stability by steering the rear wheels in the opposite direction of the front wheels at low speeds and in the same direction at high speeds.


Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM) : Electronically adjusts the damping force based on driving conditions and driver input.


Porsche Torque Vectoring Plus (PTV Plus): Enhances cornering performance by applying brake force to the inside rear wheel and working with an electronically controlled rear differential lock.


EXTERIOR


Design: Aggressive and purposeful design with wider bodywork, large rear wing, and extensive use of carbon fiber for weight reduction.


Materials: Lightweight materials such as carbon fiber-reinforced plastic (CFRP) for the roof, hood, and fenders


Lighting: LED headlights and taillights with optional Matrix LED technology.


INTERIOR


Seats: Full bucket seats made of carbon fiber for weight reduction and enhanced support.


Technology: Minimalistic design focused on driving experience, with Porsche Communication Management (PCM) including a high-resolution touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, and optional BOSE or Burmester sound systems.


Instrumentation: Digital and analog displays, including a central analog tachometer with digital side displays for speed and other vehicle information.


Upholstery: Race-Tex materials with contrasting stitching, and extensive use of lightweight materials.
